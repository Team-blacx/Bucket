Description
About Bucket

Bucket is a private, decentralised messaging app with no central servers. Your device IS the server — messages travel peer-to-peer through the Tor network using your unique onion address.

Unlike WhatsApp or Telegram, Bucket stores nothing anywhere. No phone number, no email, no account creation. Just scan a QR code and start chatting with complete anonymity.

Built for journalists, activists, and anyone who believes a conversation should remain between two people — and no one else. Developed by Blacx-Tech.
