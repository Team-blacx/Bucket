## 🔒 What is Bucket?

**Bucket** is a fully decentralized, anonymous messaging app that uses the **Tor network** for peer-to-peer communication. There are **no central servers**, no cloud, no phone numbers, and no accounts. Your device IS the server.

Unlike WhatsApp or Telegram, Bucket stores nothing anywhere. Messages travel directly from your device to your contact's device through encrypted Tor circuits.

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🌐 **Tor Network** | Every message routes through Tor — your real IP is never exposed |
| 📷 **QR Identity** | Add contacts by scanning QR codes — no phone number needed |
| ⏱ **Auto-Delete** | Messages self-destruct on your customizable timer |
| 🔄 **Circuit Refresh** | New Tor circuit on-demand for fresh routing |
| 🛡️ **On-Device Keys** | Cryptographic keys generated and stored locally |
| 🔒 **Zero Servers** | No backend, no cloud — your phone handles everything |

## 📦 Quick Start

1. **Download** the [latest APK](https://team-blacx.github.io/Bucket/Bucket-v2_7-release.apk)
2. **Install** — Allow "unknown apps" in your device settings
3. **Launch** — Tor auto-connects (green dot = you're private)
4. **Share your QR** — Tap "My QR" and share only with trusted contacts
5. **Scan & Chat** — When both online, encrypted chat begins
